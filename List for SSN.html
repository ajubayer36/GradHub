List of Documents Required

Download Official Checklist (PDF)
  • Application Form (Form SS-5)
  • Proof of Eligibility (e.g., F-1 or J-1 visa, I-20 or DS-2019)
  • Proof of Identity (e.g., Passport and any other ID)
  • Proof of Age (e.g., Birth Certificate)
  • Form I-94 (Arrival/Departure Record)
  • Employment Authorization (e.g., EAD, OPT card)
  • Letter from School's DSO or RO
