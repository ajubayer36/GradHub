Opening A Bank Account

Type of Accounts

  • Saving: A savings account is designed for individuals to save money and earn interest on their savings. It typically has limitations on the number of withdrawals and transfers you can make each month.
  • Checking: A checking account is for day-to-day transactions. It allows you to write checks, make electronic payments, and withdraw money through debit cards or ATMs. Checking accounts are used for routine expenses.
