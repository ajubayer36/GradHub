Honors College Fellowship

Annual Award Eligibility Criteria Application Procedure Renewal Criteria Apply
$20,000 per year 32 ACT/1430 SAT and 3.90 GPA or higher Requires application for admission along with the Honors College Fellowship application
Priority Deadline: November 15 (Scholarship Priority Consideration Deadline)
Final Deadline: February 1		 Cumulative 3.00 GPA and 27 hours earned by the end of the second semester of each academic year, and 30 hours earned in each subsequent academic year.
