State Tax Filing Instructions and Forms

Arkansas

Here are the state tax filing instructions and forms for Arkansas:
Arkansas State Tax Form: AR1000 - Individual Income Tax Return
Filing Deadline: Generally April 15th
Instructions: You can find the detailed instructions for filling out the AR1000 form on the Arkansas Department of Finance and Administration website.

Texas

Here are the state tax filing instructions and forms for Texas:
Texas State Tax Form: 2020 - Texas Individual Income Tax Return
Filing Deadline: Generally April 15th
Instructions: Detailed instructions for the Texas 2020 Individual Income Tax Return can be found on the Texas Comptroller of Public Accounts website.
